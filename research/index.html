Gender bias in neuro citations

A study of gendered citation practices within top neuroscience journals. Shows overall undercitation of women-led work, particularly by men-led teams.

Published in Biostatistics
Topics: Nonparametric stats, MRI data
Skills: Statistical methods, Simulation
